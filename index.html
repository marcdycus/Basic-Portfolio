About Me

I grew up in a small town in central Arkansas. That's where most of my family still lives, and always has lived for as long as, even my grandparents, know. I moved to Kansas City, Missouri for my art degree when I graduated high school, and after saving up some money, I moved to New York.

I have a 50 pound goldendoodle named Nora and two cats named Florence and Olive. They are my fur children, and they're also Arkansans.

I've been bartending ever since I moved to New York, and I've been teaching myself how to code on the side as much as I have time. I hope to soon transition to a career path that allows me to enhance what knowledge I have and will have.
